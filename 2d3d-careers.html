Description

We’re looking for a 2D/3D Graphic Artist/ Animator to join our growing creative team. In this role, you'll be instrumental in designing and crafting engaging animations and visual content for slot games, online gaming platforms, casino experiences, and fintech applications. Your work will bring life to interactive digital environments and elevate both gameplay and user interface experiences. If you're passionate about animation and motion design in the world of digital entertainment—and excited to be part of a fast-moving, creative team—we’d love to hear from you!

Key Responsibilities

  • • Design and create high-quality animations and visual assets for characters, objects, environments, and UI elements.
  • • Work closely with game designers, developers, and other artists to ensure seamless integration of animations into interactive experiences.
  • • Prototype and develop animation systems that contribute to feature planning and user experience.
  • • Create motion graphics, video assets, and UI animations that align with brand identity and enhance user engagement.
  • • Troubleshoot and resolve animation-related issues, optimizing for performance without compromising quality.
  • • Maintain consistency in motion design style and visual storytelling across multiple projects.
  • • Meet deadlines while managing multiple tasks and adapting to project changes.
  • • Stay updated on industry tools, trends, and animation techniques to continuously improve visual quality and innovation.

Qualifications:

  • • Proficiency in 2D and 3D animation software, including Blender, Unity, Spine 2D, and the Adobe Creative Suite (After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator).
  • • Experience developing motion assets for interactive digital platforms (games, apps, or web).
  • • Strong artistic sense with understanding of color theory, timing, typography, and composition.
  • • A solid portfolio showcasing a range of animation work in gaming, UI motion design, and storytelling.
  • • Experience in optimizing animation assets for different devices and platforms.
  • • Excellent communication skills in English (written and spoken).
  • • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative, and creative environment.
  • • Highly organized, detail-oriented, and able to handle multiple projects simultaneously.
  • • Familiarity with game development pipelines and agile workflows.
